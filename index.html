Welcome to my portfolio website. This website shows and explains some of the projects I completed during this school year, including my Screen-printing, Genetics, and Black Box projects. Each project helped me develop new skills and gain a better understanding of different subjects through hands-on learning and problem-solving. In screen printing, I learned how to create designs, prepare screens, apply ink, and print images onto different materials. These projects taught me creativity, patience, and attention to detail. In the genetics project, I learned about DNA and genetic testing. We watched videos, collected DNA samples by swabbing our mouths, prepared the samples for testing, and analyzed the results. We also completed interviews before and after receiving our results to compare our predictions with the actual data. Through the Black Box project, I worked on investigating what was inside a closed box without opening it. By making observations, testing different possibilities, and analyzing evidence, I learned how scientists use indirect evidence to develop explanations and solve problems. I created this website using GitHub Pages to publish my work online and W3Schools to help me learn and use HTML and CSS. While building this website, I learned how to create webpages, add images and videos, make navigation links, and customize the appearance of my site with CSS. This website allows me to show and explain my work and reflect on the skills and knowledge I gained throughout the year.
