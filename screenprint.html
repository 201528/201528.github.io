My Screenprinting Journey

During this Screenprinting project, we went over to Adobe Photoshop and we started to think of ideas of what we would do our project on. We then got our idea and started by making our rough draft. Once we finalized our picture we went over to Adobe illustrator where we started making our measurements so that when we go to print it will look good. Then we used Silhouette Studio to print our final idea, we then went over to the physical part of this project where we used nails, glue, tape, wood, and mesh to make our frame and once we finished that, we then used a heat press to press our idea on the frame. After we did that we went to the printing part where we physically printed our idea using a squeegee to print our final idea on a paper, box, and fabric.

What I learned

During this screen-printing project, I learned a lot about the process of creating a design and turning it into a finished product. I learned how to prepare a screen, apply ink correctly, and print a design onto different things. At first, some parts of the process were difficult because I had to be very careful and precise. If the screen was not lined up correctly or too much ink was used, the print would not come out the way I wanted. As I worked on the project, I learned how to fix mistakes, pay attention to details, and improve my technique. I also learned that screen printing requires patience and practice because getting a clean, high-quality print takes time. By the end of the project, I had a much better understanding of how screen printing works and felt proud of the final product I created.

21st Century Skill

The 21st-century skill where I showed the most growth was perseverance. There were several times when things did not go exactly as planned, but I kept working and making improvements instead of giving up. I also showed responsibility by using the equipment properly, following directions, and completing my work on time. Through this project, I learned that mistakes are a normal part of learning and can help you improve your skills. This experience helped me become more confident in my abilities and taught me the importance of staying focused and working through challenges until I reach my goal.
